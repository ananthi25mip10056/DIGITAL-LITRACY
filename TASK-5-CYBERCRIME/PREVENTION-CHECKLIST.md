PREVENTION CHECKLIST

# General Safety Tips
1. Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Always verify the identity of unknown callers claiming to be from banks or companies.
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading apps from unknown sources.
4. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
5. Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi, especially for financial transactions.
6. Regularly check your bank account for any unauthorized transactions.

# UPI & Financial Safety Tips
7. Remember that a UPI PIN is required only for sending money, not receiving it.
8. Do not approve unknown payment requests or scan QR codes from untrusted sources.

# Reporting Cybercrime
9. Report incidents immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline Number: 1930 for immediate assistance.
