# Email 2: Internship Inquiry
Write an email from a student to an internship coordinator at a company,
expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity.


**Subject:** Inquiry Regarding Summer Internship Opportunity  

Dear Sir,  

I hope this message finds you well. My name is Kumari Shikha Kushwaha, and I am currently a student at VIT Bhopal pursuing my studies in Computer Science. I am writing to express my interest in a summer internship opportunity at your organization.  

I am eager to gain practical experience and enhance my technical and professional skills in a real-world environment. I am particularly interested in applying my academic knowledge to industry-based projects and learning from experienced professionals.  

I would be grateful if you could provide me with information regarding any available internship opportunities and the application process. I would be happy to share my resume and any additional details if required.  

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to your response.  

Yours sincerely,  
**Ananthi T**  
Currently @VIT Bhopal
